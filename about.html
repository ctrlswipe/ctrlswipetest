WHO IS CTRL Swipe?

TUDOR & DIDI // FOUNDERS

CTRL Swipe is a social media marketing agency from Baia Mare, Romania, built to help small and medium-sized businesses communicate clearly, consistently and professionally online. We combine creative production with strategic thinking to deliver content that looks good, sounds right and performs well across Facebook, Instagram and TikTok.

We are a compact two-person team, Tudor and Didi, which means every client works directly with the people who plan, create, film, edit and manage their content. No layers, no delays. Just a streamlined process and personal involvement in every step.

We create full online ecosystems for brands: static posts, branded graphics, short-form videos, captions, ad campaigns and monthly on-site filming sessions in northern Romania. In parallel, we offer complete remote social media management for brands worldwide, including planning, design, copywriting, scheduling and maintaining a consistent content flow, regardless of location.

Our approach is built on clarity, aesthetics, speed and honesty. Every project starts with understanding the client’s identity and ends with content that strengthens it. Our mission is simple: help businesses grow through modern storytelling, clean visuals and a structured, reliable workflow that takes social media off their shoulders, whether they are nearby or across the globe.
